Bio

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at ShanghaiTech University Visual Intelligent Center (VIC), supervised by Prof. Jingyi Yu. Before that, I received my Bachelor degree in 2016 from Xidian University.

My research interests lie at the intersection of computer graphics and computer vision, including image synthesis/editing, geometric modeling, and realistic rendering. I have a great passion on new things and ideas, my goal is to create magic and happiness. Outside my research, I love photography, badminton, and movie appreciation.

I'll graduate at the end of 2021 and currently looking for a postdotoral position, feel free to reach me if you are interested in me.
